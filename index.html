About Us

MONEROMINEco was founded in February 2021, essentially as a non-profit. Our goal was to not only provide a mining pool to aid in hashrate decentralization, but a hub for the community to learn and discuss Monero (XMR). Since then we've pushed to implement additional Monero-focused projects, starting with a public node for wallets, and will continue rolling features out into the forseeable future. We are staffed with an excellent team, and our community's growth & passion has been astounding! Please feel free to join our Discord community to discuss cryptocurrency at large, view MONEROMINEco announcements, and participate in events/giveaways. You can also check out our Wiki, which contains our feature roadmap and a slew of information on Monero, Mining, our Pool, and more.
